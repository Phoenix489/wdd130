“The Stranger: ‘Friend or Foe’” 2022, 24” x 18”, colored pencil, An adaptation of Frederic Remington’s “The Scout”, A Native American scout uses the cover of night to scout out a recently founded town, full of strangers to these lands.

“The Gamble” 2023, Procreate, 8.5” x 11”, A trio of outlaw brothers travel around the old West, causing mischief and trouble. Poker is their latest venture.

“The Sheriff and His Deputy” 2023, Photoshop, 11”x17”, After getting lost in a doggy daycare, a toy sheriff takes on one of the young puppies as his deputy. An unlikely duo forms and gets themselves into trouble.

“Covering The New Frontier” 2024, Photoshop, 9”x12”, A conceptual illustration about how the United States government’s forced removal of over 60,000 Native Americans from their homes during the time of 1830 - 1850 was unethical, unfair, and cruel.
